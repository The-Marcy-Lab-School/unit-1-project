According to the Met Office, there are 3 main causes for climate change:

  • A leading cause is burning fossil fuels like oil, gas and coal.
  • Deforestation plaugues the environment as well, eliminating trees that would otherwise absorb carbon dioxide.
  • Agricultural depletion as well since animals produce methane, which is 30 times more powerful than carbon dioxide.

These in term lead to the melting of sea ice, which helps reflect heat. However, as more sea ice melts, the area of dark surfaces (ie. ocean and land) increases and absorbs more heat.
