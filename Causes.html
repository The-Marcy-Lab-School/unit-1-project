The main causes of climate change is burning fossil fuels like oil, gas and coal. A few other main player in climate change would be deforestation, Deforestation eliminates trees that would other wise absorb carbon dioxide. On top of that trees when burned release carbon dioxide. Agriculture is another key player as animals produce methane, which is 30 times more powerful than carbon dioxide. These the top 3 causes for climate change according to the Met office. All of these contribute to the increase of greenhouse gases that in a time trap heat and increases the world temperature. This in term leads to the melting of sea ice, which helps reflect heat, but as more sea ice melts the area of dark surfaces (ie, ocean and land) increases and this absorbs more heat.
