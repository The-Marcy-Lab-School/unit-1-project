What drives Climate change?

The number of drivers in climate change are numerous from man made causes to natural catalyst. The top 3 are:

The burning of fossil fuels

These fuel sources contain carbon dioxide that has been stored underground for centries. When burend, the stored carbon dioxide is relased into the air.

Deforestation

Deforestation eliminates trees that would other wise absorb carbon dioxide. On top of that trees when burned release carbon dioxide.

Agriculture

Agriculture is another key player as animals produce methane, which is 30 times more powerful than carbon dioxide.
