What is climate change?

The long-term shift in global or regional climate patterns. The context is frequently used to refer to the rise in global temperatures from the mid-twentieth century to the present.

Why is it important?

According to our research, we have a very limited amount of time left to undo the damage that has happened to our society from climate change. As the climate increases, our resources will deplete over time, causing it to live harder in the world we have.

Our Mission

Our goal is to bring awareness to the public about this how this is an essential problem and it can be contained.
